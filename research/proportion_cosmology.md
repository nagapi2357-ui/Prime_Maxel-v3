# Proportion as Cosmology
**Date:** 2026-04-06
**Author:** Adrian (Tusk)

## The Four Orientations of the Fraction

| Form | Meaning |
|------|---------|
| 1/1  | The Whole — unity, undivided |
| x/1  | Macrocosm — expansion outward, sequential reference |
| 1/x  | Microcosm — contraction inward |
| x/y  | Relationship — the ratio between two elements, imbued with meaning |

## Core Insight

**Proportion is prior to number.** 

- `x/1` or `y/1` is merely a sequential reference — an address on a line
- `x/y` carries the *relationship* within it — self-contained meaning
- The ratio **is** the information

`x/y` is the general form; unity (1/1), macro (x/1), and micro (1/x) are all special cases — boundary conditions of proportion.

## Connections

- **Great Pyramid:** 14:11 seked ratio simultaneously encodes π and Φ — the relationship, not the absolute measurements, carries the information
- **Music:** Harmony exists in ratios (3:2, 4:3) — individual frequencies are arbitrary, the relationship is what you hear
- **Φ:** Defined purely as proportion — whole:part :: part:remainder — ratio all the way down
- **Primes:** Resist ratio (exist only as x/1) — they're the irreducible elements from which all ratios are built, standing outside proportion. The scaffolding.
- **Material vs Supernatural:** Primes (sequential, absolute) vs Ratios (relational, proportional) — the fundamental prime pair from "The Shedding"
